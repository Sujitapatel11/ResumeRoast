Part 8 – Community Intelligence
1. Vision
Community Intelligence transforms individual interview experiences into verified, structured
knowledge that benefits every CareerOS user.
2. Objectives
• Build a trusted interview knowledge base.
• Reduce misinformation.
• Continuously improve AI recommendations.
• Capture real hiring trends.
3. Contribution Workflow
User submits interview experience
fi AI validation
fi Spam detection
fi Duplicate detection
fi Similarity check
fi Confidence scoring
fi Community voting
fi Moderator approval
fi Published to Knowledge Base
4. Contribution Types
Interview experiences
Online assessment questions
Hiring timelines
Salary insights
Resume feedback
Company-specific preparation tips
5. Trust & Reputation
Every contributor has a reputation score based on:
• Approved submissions
• Community votes
• Report accuracy
• Account history
Higher reputation increases contribution weight.
6. Anti-Fake Strategy
Require structured forms instead of free text.
Detect duplicates with semantic similarity.
Flag suspicious activity.
Rate-limit submissions.
Allow community reporting and moderator review.
7. Knowledge Base
Store structured records by:
Company
Role
Location
Experience level
Interview round
Topic
Difficulty
Date
Confidence score
8. AI Integration
AI summarizes verified community data and uses it to personalize roadmaps, interview preparation
and company insights. Unverified data is never treated as authoritative.
9. Moderation
Admin tools:
Pending queue
Flag review
User suspension
Content editing
Appeal workflow
10. Open Source Strategy
Documentation, templates and non-sensitive tooling remain open source.
Sensitive production data, user information and verified knowledge remain protected.
11. CTO Decision Record
Accepted:
Structured contributions
Reputation system
AI-assisted moderation
Human review for publication
Rejected:
Anonymous publishing
Unverified reports
Public editing without controls
