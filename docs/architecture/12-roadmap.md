11. Long-Term Vision (3–5 Years)
Become the default Career Operating System for students, professionals, universities and
recruiters through personalized AI, trusted community knowledge and measurable career progress.
12. Final CTO Commitment
Every new feature must be evaluated against this blueprint.
If a feature does not improve the user's probability of getting hired or fit the architecture, it will not
be implemented.
The blueprint evolves through versioning, but architectural consistency remains the guiding
principle
