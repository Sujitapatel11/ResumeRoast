CareerOS Master Blueprint
Part 2 – Product Design & User Experience
1. Product Goal
Design a guided hiring workflow instead of a collection of AI tools. Every screen should move the
user one step closer to getting hired.
2. Primary User Personas
• Final-year student
• Fresh graduate
• Career switcher
• Junior engineer
• Experienced engineer preparing for top companies.
3. Complete User Journey
Create Account fi Career Profile fi Upload Resume fi Resume Intelligence fi Resume
Improvement fi Target Company fi Company Intelligence fi Skill Gap fi Roadmap fi Learning fi
Mock Interview fi Feedback fi Applications fi Community Intelligence fi Offer.
4. Career Graph
CareerOS continuously links resumes, skills, projects, interviews, applications and learning
progress into one evolving career profile.
5. Product Modules

Resume Intelligence
Company Intelligence

AI Platform
6. Resume Intelligence
Upload PDF, ATS Score, Resume Roast, Missing Skills, Resume Rewrite, Resume Versions,
Export PDF/DOCX.
7. Company Intelligence
Company profile, hiring trends, required skills, interview rounds, salary insights, company-specific
optimization.
8. Roadmap Engine
Generate personalized 30/60/90-day plans using skill gaps, target role and available study time.
9. Learning Engine
Recommend official docs, courses, videos, projects and practice questions ranked by quality.
10. Interview Engine
Coding, HR, Behavioral and System Design mock interviews with AI feedback and weak-topic
detection.
11. Application Tracker
Track wishlist, applications, OA, interviews, offers, rejections and automatically update the roadmap
after each outcome.
12. Community Intelligence
Structured interview reports, hiring signals, salary insights, confidence scoring, moderation and
contributor reputation.
13. Trust & Reputation
AI validation fi Spam detection fi Duplicate detection fi Community voting fi Moderator approval
fi Knowledge Base.
14. Subscription Strategy
Free: Resume analysis and basic features.
Pro: Unlimited optimization, interviews, roadmap and analytics.
Enterprise: Universities and placement cells.
15. Product Design Rules
• Workflow over chat.
• One source of truth.
• Explain every recommendation.
• Measure progress.
• AI assists decisions; it is not the product
